Core platform
Everything your operation
needs in one system

Cannabis compliance isn't a feature you bolt on — it's the foundation. Borealis is built around the reporting, traceability, and audit requirements your state program demands.

Seed-to-sale plant tracking
RFID and barcode tagging from clone to harvest with full chain-of-custody at every stage. Every plant accounted for — always.
Metrc two-way sync
Automatic transfers, manifests, and lab result uploads. BorealisCore is your system of record — Metrc receives compliance writes as a side effect, not the other way around.
Lab COA automation
Certificates of analysis attach to batches automatically. Inventory holds until results clear — no one has to remember to check.
Retail POS built in (roadmap)
Compliant point-of-sale with age verification, purchase limits, and full traceability to the consumer. No separate POS integration required.
Multi-state from day one
State-specific Metrc endpoints and rules live in configuration, not code. Expanding to a new state is a data change, not a deployment.
