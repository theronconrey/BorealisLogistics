Our Approach

Built on open principles.

We believe operators shouldn't be locked into black-box software. BorealisCore is open source, API-first, and designed to last.

Open Source First

The full codebase is public. Audit it, fork it, contribute to it. No vendor lock-in.

Cloud + On-Premise

Deploy to the cloud or run it locally. Your data, your infrastructure, your call.

API-First

Every function is available via API. Integrate with anything, extend everything.

Offline Capable

POS and inventory keep working when the internet doesn't. Syncs automatically on reconnect.
